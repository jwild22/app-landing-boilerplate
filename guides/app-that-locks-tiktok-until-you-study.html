An app that locks TikTok until you study

Exam Master Guides · Updated July 2026

If you searched this, you already know the problem: TikTok opens itself. One tap during a study break and the algorithm keeps you for an hour. You don't need another lecture about screen time — you need the app physically locked until the work is done.

That app exists. Exam Master blocks TikTok (and Instagram, YouTube, or any app you pick) on iPhone, and the only key is answering study questions correctly. Here's the full setup, start to finish.

How the lock actually works

Exam Master uses Apple's Screen Time framework — the same system parental controls use. When you block an app, iOS itself enforces the shield. That matters for two reasons:

  • It holds when Exam Master is closed. The block isn't a flimsy overlay or a VPN trick; it's enforced by the operating system.
  • Your data stays on your device. Apple's framework never tells the app what you do inside blocked apps, and Exam Master's developer states no personal data is collected.

When you try to open a blocked app, you hit a shield instead of the feed. To get past it, you earn time — by studying.

Set it up in 5 minutes

Step 1: Install and grant Screen Time access

Download Exam Master from the App Store (free). On first launch it asks for Screen Time permission — this is the iOS-level authorization that makes real blocking possible. Approve it once and you're done.

Step 2: Pick the apps to lock

Select TikTok, plus whatever else derails you — Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts are the usual suspects. Leave messaging apps unblocked so staying reachable never becomes your excuse to unlock everything.

Step 3: Build a Question Bank

This is what you'll study to earn access. Type any topic and get a generated quiz, or paste your own notes, photos, or PDFs and Exam Master turns them into flashcards. Make it the subject you're actually being tested on — biology, driving theory, bar prep, vocabulary.

Step 4: Set your rate and start earning

Choose how many minutes each correct answer is worth. From then on, the loop is simple: urge to scroll → take a quiz → correct answers add minutes → minutes unlock TikTok. Wrong answers add nothing. When your earned time runs out, the shield comes back.

Tip: Set the rate so a solid 10-question quiz earns about 15–20 minutes. Big enough to feel worth it, small enough that a whole evening of scrolling requires a whole evening's worth of studying.

Why this beats a plain app blocker

Standard blockers (Screen Time limits, focus apps) share one weakness: the "ignore limit" button. When the only options are blocked or give up, you eventually give up. Exam Master gives you a third option — earn it — which is why the block survives contact with a stressful Tuesday night. You're never locked out of TikTok forever; you're just asked to study first. Study harder, scroll more.
