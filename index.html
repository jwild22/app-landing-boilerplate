How it works

Your distractions become your reward

The deal is simple: the apps that eat your day stay locked until you prove you studied. No willpower required — iOS does the blocking for you.

1

Block your distracting apps

Pick the apps that pull you away from studying — TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, games. Exam Master locks them with Apple's Screen Time framework, so the block holds even when the app is closed.

2

Answer quiz questions

Build a Question Bank in under 60 seconds: type a topic, or paste notes, photos, or PDFs and get flashcards instantly. Then quiz yourself — spaced repetition brings back what you're about to forget.

3

Earn your screen time

Every correct answer earns minutes for the apps you locked. Wrong answers earn nothing. You decide how much each answer is worth — then study your way back to your feed.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does Exam Master block distracting apps?

Exam Master uses Apple's Screen Time framework to lock the apps you choose — TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, games, anything. They stay locked until you answer quiz questions correctly. Blocking runs through iOS itself, so it holds even when Exam Master is closed. Learn more

How do I earn screen time by studying?

You set how many minutes each correct answer is worth. Then you take a quiz from one of your Question Banks: every correct answer adds minutes to your balance, and that earned time unlocks your blocked apps. Wrong answers earn nothing, so real studying is the only way in. Learn more

Can Exam Master turn my notes or PDFs into flashcards?

Yes. Paste text, upload photos of your notes, or import a PDF, and Exam Master generates flashcards and quiz questions from it automatically. You can also type a topic and get a ready-made quiz in seconds. Learn more

What is brainrot, and does Exam Master actually help?

Brainrot is the mental fog that comes from hours of short-form video and endless scrolling. Exam Master helps by flipping the incentive: the scrolling apps stay locked until you study, so the dopamine loop that kept you scrolling now pushes you to learn. Learn more

Does Exam Master use spaced repetition?

Yes. Questions you get wrong come back sooner, and questions you know well come back later — right before you would forget them. That schedule moves facts into long-term memory far more efficiently than rereading notes. Learn more

Which exams can I prepare for with Exam Master?

Any exam you can study with flashcards: SAT, ACT, MCAT, NCLEX, ASVAB, IELTS, DMV permit tests, AP exams, finals, vocabulary, and more. You build Question Banks for your exact material or generate them from your own notes. Learn more

Is Exam Master free?

Exam Master is free to download and try. A subscription ($7.99/week or $59.99/year) unlocks the full experience. There's no account required, and the developer states that no personal data is collected. Get it on the App Store

How is Exam Master different from a normal app blocker?

Normal app blockers only say no — they block apps on a timer and rely on your willpower not to hit "ignore." Exam Master gives you a way to earn access: answer questions correctly and you unlock minutes. Blocking plus a reward loop is what makes it stick. Learn more
